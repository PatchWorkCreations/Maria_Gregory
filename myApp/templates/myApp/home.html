The Woman Behind the Mission

Maria Gregory is a storyteller with a gift for seeing strength in others long before they see it in themselves — a visionary who builds communities, nurtures people, and turns compassion into action.

As the founder of the I RiseUp Group of Companies—including I RiseUp Academy, I RiseUp Foundation, and CLICK: Computer Learning for Inner City Kids—Maria has dedicated her life to creating pathways for people to grow, heal, and discover their inner lion.

Her work is rooted in a simple but profound belief: every person carries greatness inside them — they just need someone to remind them it's there.
