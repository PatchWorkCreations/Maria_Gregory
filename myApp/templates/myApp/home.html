The Guide Behind the Guides

"I'm the person leaders speak to when they've outgrown generic advice and need someone who can meet them quietly, honestly, and without judgment.

Most of the people I walk with don't 'need' help in the traditional sense. They're capable, seasoned, and deeply self-reliant. What they want is a place where their insight, strength, and humanity can all exist at the same time — without being filtered through a title."

"Along the way, I've founded and served in initiatives focused on growth, healing, and education — including the I RiseUp group of organizations and community-focused programs for youth.

Today, my calling is more focused: to walk alongside the people who walk alongside everyone else."

"Even the most self-sufficient leaders deserve a place where they don't have to hold everything on their own."
